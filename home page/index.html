Bio

I grew up far away from the city. I wasn't just in a small town, I was outside of a small town. This was the sort of place where the most advanced tech was probably some sort of corn harvester. With my school's limited computer classes, it's sort of a mystery as to why I'm here at RIT for a computer-related major. All I can say is, it's nice to finally be somewhere where people know what a VM is and why recursion needs an exit condition... It was pretty lonely back home when my only relatable friend was an old laptop that could barely play Minecraft.
